Our Story

A Family Table,
An Elevated Experience

Red Tavern is a family-owned and operated restaurant rooted in the belief that exceptional hospitality and extraordinary cuisine can coexist with genuine warmth. Our owners — alongside our chef and sous chef — bring decades of professional culinary training to every plate.

We describe our cuisine as Upscale New American with deep European influences: a living menu that changes with the seasons, sourced from local farms and artisan producers who share our commitment to quality.

Our cocktail program honors both classic Prohibition-era craft and modern seasonal invention. Our award-winning wine list spans boutique California producers to celebrated vineyards from Italy, France, Portugal, Spain, Austria, and Israel.

— The Red Tavern Family
